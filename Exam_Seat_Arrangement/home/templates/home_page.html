Features of Our System

Automated Seat Allocation

Efficiently assigns students to seats across classrooms based on your input, eliminating manual seat arrangement tasks.

Flexible Classroom Configurations

Supports various classroom layouts by allowing customization of columns, rows, and benches per row for each room.

PDF Export

Generate and download a PDF of seating arrangements, organized for easy printing, with each classroom on a separate page.

Secure & Easy Access

Our platform offers a user-friendly, secure environment for managing seat arrangements with access control through user login.
